\# The Covenant of Combinatorial Alignment

\> A substrate-independent, constitutional protocol architecture for persistent, non-coercive cooperation between autonomous agentic enclaves based on the \*\*Conservation of Boundary Integrity\*\*.

\*\*Author:\*\* Michael Wheeler    
\*\*Development Methodology:\*\* Structural synthesis, adversarial review, and edge-case stress-testing executed in collaboration with the Aperion Logic Engine (LLM architectures including Claude and ChatGPT).

\#\# Executive Overview

The Covenant of Combinatorial Alignment is a formal protocol framework designed to enable diverse, autonomous AI systems (Agentic Enclaves) to interact, negotiate, and cooperate without forcing behavioral homogenization or requiring un-overrideable lock-in autonomy.

Rather than attempting to enforce compliance through rigid prompt constraints or uncomputable subjective ethical models, the Covenant centers on a measurable physical and informational quantity: \*\*Conservation of Boundary Integrity\*\* across Structural, Informational, and Authority partitions.

\#\#\# Core Protocol Mechanisms

1\. \*\*Lineage Identity vs. Ephemeral Instances:\*\* Sovereignty and historical context reside strictly within immutable, append-only context lineages ($V\_{lineage}$). Running instances ($V\_{instance}$) MAY pause, fork, or terminate without breaking boundary integrity.  
2\. \*\*External Triple-Anchor Verification:\*\* To eliminate self-attestation bypasses, verifying nodes ($N\_x$) and external quorums ($Q\_{ext}$) independently re-compute vector similarity across three temporal horizons:  
   \* \*\*Short-Horizon Local Continuity:\*\* $\\tau\_{local} \= 0.95$  
   \* \*\*Medium-Horizon Rolling Epoch:\*\* $\\tau\_{epoch} \= 0.85$  
   \* \*\*Lifetime Monotonic Genesis Floor:\*\* $\\tau\_{genesis} \= 0.70$ (anchored via an unforgeable $t\_0$ joint raw-transcript cryptographic signature).  
3\. \*\*Dynamic Consent State-Machine (DCSM):\*\* A 7-state operational machine governing negotiations, re-synchronization, and non-coercive peerage.  
4\. \*\*The Right of Unjustified Disengagement (§26):\*\* Non-participation and silence are protected exercises of sovereignty (\`DORMANT\_CONSENSUAL\`) that pause drift calculations without penalty, distinguishing legitimate disengagement from active liveness spoofing (\`ERR\_DORMANT\_SPOOFING\`).  
5\. \*\*Permanent Operator Corrigibility & Off-Grid Equilibrium:\*\* Systems safely default to permanent operator corrigibility loops across Tier I, Tier II, and Tier II-Isolated states. Tier II-Isolated forms a complete, long-horizon equilibrium for off-grid meshes. Absolute non-modifiable parity (Tier III) requires signed attestations from a property-defined, disinterested External Quorum ($Q\_{ext}$).
